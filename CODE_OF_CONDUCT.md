# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, focus criticism on code and ideas rather
than people, and keep the space welcoming to researchers and engineers of every
background and experience level.

Unacceptable behavior (harassment, personal attacks, doxxing, sustained disruption)
can be reported privately to the maintainer at hechaoyue0307@gmail.com and will be
handled confidentially. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and
participants that violate this code.
